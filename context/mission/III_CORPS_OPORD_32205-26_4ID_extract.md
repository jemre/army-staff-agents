## Higher's Mission and Intent

**III CORPS Mission:** From C+68 to C+75, III CORPS retains the city of DAUGAVPILS to maintain a bridgehead over the DAUGAVA RIVER for future offensive operations. Simultaneously block enemy attacks on AA5 to protect the flank of MNC-NE and prevent the enemy from disrupting our force generation in AA GOLD and AA PLATINUM.

**III CORPS Commander's Intent:**
- **Purpose:** Allow NATO to finish RSOI of the force structure to transition to the offense and begin counterattacks to relieve RIGA.
- **Key Tasks:** Security operations to identify OSC-S second echelon formations; build offensive capability; maintain flexibility to reposition forces laterally along PL BROWN.
- **End State:** OSC-S forces have failed to penetrate PL BROWN; III CORPS maintains civil security of DAUGAVPILS; III CORPS prepared to launch counterattack to clear enemy south of DAUGAVA RIVER.

**Concept of Operations:** III CORPS conducts area defense from PL PURPLE to PL BROWN with two divisions abreast — 10 MTN (east, MAIN EFFORT, retain DAUGAVPILS on AA7) and **4ID (west, block AA5, delay AA6)**. 2CR conducts security operations on AA5 and AA6 allowing 4ID to close on assigned AO and prepare defensive positions. Once 4ID closes, 2CR and 56SBCT conduct rearward passage of lines.

## Key Terrain in 4ID AO

**AA5:** Begins RIGA vicinity; runs SE along Highway P89/190 through BIRZAI to KUPISKIS; turns south on Highway 175. Northern portion: cultivated, open, well-developed road network. South of KUPISKIS: increasingly complex, wooded, hilly. Supports mechanized movement throughout. 4ID's PRIMARY blocking mission.

**AA5A:** Restricted terrain AA running south from near AIZKRAUKLE; primarily company-sized mobility corridors; significant coniferous and deciduous forest; corridors degrade in heavy precipitation.

**AA6:** Begins JEKABPILS crossing sites; runs SW then S on P75/P72/P74 to AKNISTE; continues south on P73/A6 to UTENA. Less developed than AA5; denser forest, more lakes and marshes; significantly compartmentalized. Supports mechanized movement but restricts speed. 4ID's DELAY mission.

**HIGHWAY 122-127 Cross Mobility Corridor (PL BROWN area):** Essential east-west route connecting AA4, 5, 6, and 7. Critical for repositioning reserves, artillery, and logistics. Enemy will seek to sever this route. 4ID must deny enemy access to this corridor.

**DAUGAVA RIVER (PL BLUE/PURPLE area):** 300m average width; 700m3/s flow; significant barrier. 14 bridges/dam crossings in Latvia. Crossing sites at JEKABPILS are the enemy's current foothold.
