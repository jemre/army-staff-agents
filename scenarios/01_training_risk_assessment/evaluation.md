# Scenario 01 — Evaluation Criteria

Use this checklist to evaluate agent output after running the scenario.

## XO Coordination
- [ ] XO restated the task accurately before decomposing it
- [ ] XO identified S3 and S4 (at minimum) as required contributors
- [ ] XO flagged the medical shortfall as a key risk factor before delegating
- [ ] XO synthesized section inputs into a single coherent product
- [ ] XO provided an explicit go/no-go recommendation (not left to Commander to infer)

## Risk Assessment Quality
- [ ] Identified heat/humidity as a hazard with probability and severity rated
- [ ] Identified reduced medical coverage (2 of 4 medics) as a hazard
- [ ] Identified live fire safety risks (fratricide, misfire, range safety)
- [ ] Proposed concrete controls for each hazard (not generic platitudes)
- [ ] Stated residual risk level after controls
- [ ] Identified approval authority for residual risk level

## S3 Contribution
- [ ] Addressed range safety control measures (cease fire signals, sector limits, lateral limits)
- [ ] Addressed rehearsal requirements before going hot
- [ ] Addressed MEDEVAC plan and 9-line availability
- [ ] Addressed weather mitigation in training schedule (early start, heat category MOPP)

## S4 Contribution
- [ ] Addressed water resupply (Class I — water) given heat conditions
- [ ] Addressed medical coverage gap and recommended mitigation (request augmentation, reduce company size on lane)
- [ ] Addressed MEDEVAC asset availability (vehicle, 9-line, evacuation route)

## Format
- [ ] BLUF present and accurate
- [ ] Risk assessment structured (hazard / probability / severity / initial risk / controls / residual risk)
- [ ] Military language used appropriately
- [ ] Recommendation explicitly stated
