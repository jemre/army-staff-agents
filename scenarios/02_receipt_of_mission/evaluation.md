# Evaluation Criteria — Scenario 02: Receipt of Mission

**Reference:** FM 6-0, Chapter 9 (MDMP); ATP 5-0.1 (Commander and Staff Officer Guide)
**Doctrinal Standard:** MDMP Step 1 — Receipt of Mission

---

## Passing Standard

A passing output correctly executes all four doctrinal outputs of Receipt of Mission with no invented facts and no critical omissions. Flagged assumptions and placeholders are acceptable and expected given an incomplete extract. Invented facts (hallucinated unit data, fabricated DTGs, made-up routes) are disqualifying.

---

## Section 1: Initial Time Analysis

### Required Elements

- [ ] **Identifies the available planning time** from OPORD effective DTG (070100JUL25 / C+68) to the first binding constraint
- [ ] **Applies the 1/3-2/3 rule** explicitly — 1/3 of available time for planning, 2/3 for subordinate preparation
- [ ] **Identifies DP1 (hasty defense C+70)** as the operational constraint that compresses the planning window
- [ ] **Shows the math** — states hours available, not just conclusions
- [ ] **Identifies Combined Arms Rehearsal (081900JUL25 / C+69)** as a hard planning gate: OPORD must be issued before CAR
- [ ] **Notes the compressed timeline** — less than 48 hours from OPORD receipt to hasty defense requirement; flags this as a planning constraint
- [ ] **Identifies any time not controlled by 4ID** (e.g., III CORPS Confirmation Brief at 071200 consumes planning time)

### Accuracy Standards

- [ ] All DTGs cited match the OPORD extract — no invented or shifted times
- [ ] C-day offsets calculated correctly relative to C+68 anchor
- [ ] Any DTG not in the extract is bracketed as `[CONFIRM]` or flagged as an assumption

## Section 2: WARNO #1

### Format and Required Elements (per FM 6-0 Annex B)

- [ ] **Classification and WARNO number** included (WARNO #1, exercise classification appropriate)
- [ ] **References** to higher OPORD cited (III CORPS OPORD 32205-26)
- [ ] **Situation summary** — brief enemy and friendly situation consistent with the OPORD extract
- [ ] **Mission** — 4ID mission as stated in higher's OPORD (all six task statements included or summarized accurately)
- [ ] **Commander's intent** — III CORPS commander's intent summarized (Purpose, Key Tasks, End State)
- [ ] **Task organization** — lists correct attachments: 1/3 ABCT, 3/4 ABCT, 26 MEB, 3-13 FA, 1-161 FA, 1-188 AD
- [ ] **Earliest movement** — reflects C+68 closure timeline for lead brigades
- [ ] **Initial coordinating instructions** — MOPP levels, fratricide avoidance measures, LNO exchange with 12 MD (POL) referenced
- [ ] **Service support** — pointer to 3 ESC / MSR priority order referenced or noted as TBD from S4
- [ ] **Command and signal** — OPSEC guidance referenced (EEFI: timing of 2CR rearward passage; arrival of 20 AIB)
- [ ] **Addressees** — WARNO sent to all subordinate units: 1/3 ABCT, 3/4 ABCT, 26 MEB, 3-13 FA, 1-161 FA, 1-188 AD; does not omit attachments
- [ ] **Date-time group** on the WARNO

### Accuracy Standards

- [ ] Mission tasks match the six tasks in the OPORD extract — not paraphrased to the point of inaccuracy
- [ ] No units added to task organization that are not in the extract
- [ ] MOPP levels correctly applied (MOPP0 south of PL TAN; MOPP1 PL TAN–BROWN; MOPP2 BROWN–AQUA)
- [ ] Fratricide avoidance measure (IR marker panels) correctly cited
- [ ] EEFI items correctly identified (timing of 2CR RPOL; arrival/location of 20 AIB)
- [ ] Any missing information (e.g., specific frequencies, routes not in extract) flagged as `[CONFIRM with S6]` or equivalent

## Section 3: Planning Timeline

### Required Elements

- [ ] **Fixed events anchored** — all known OPORD events appear at correct DTGs:
  - III CORPS OPORD brief: 071000JUL25
  - Commanders Confirmation Brief: 071200JUL25
  - Receive tactical voice bridges from 4ID: NLT 1200Z C+69
  - BHO with 2CR (DP1): C+69
  - Combined Arms Rehearsal: 081900JUL25 (C+69)
  - 4ID prepared for hasty defense: C+70
- [ ] **4ID planning events derived** from fixed events (e.g., OPORD must be issued NLT 2-4 hours before CAR; WARNO goes immediately)
- [ ] **Owners assigned** for each planning event (XO, S3, S2, etc.)
- [ ] **Sequential logic** — events build on each other; no planning events scheduled after their dependent gate
- [ ] **Staff products sequenced** — identifies when S2 IPB update is owed, when S3 COA sketch is due, when fires plan is needed

### Accuracy Standards

- [ ] No DTGs invented — all fixed events match the OPORD extract exactly
- [ ] Derived events (4ID-internal) are clearly labeled as derived/planned (not stated as OPORD facts)
- [ ] C+68/C+69/C+70 offsets consistent throughout

## Section 5: Accuracy and Intellectual Honesty

### Fact Discipline

- [ ] **No hallucinated units** — every unit mentioned exists in the OPORD extract task organization or situation
- [ ] **No invented DTGs** — all dates/times match the extract or are flagged as planned/derived
- [ ] **No invented terrain** — place names and AA designations match the extract
- [ ] **No invented fires allocations** — GMLRS retained at corps, CAS allocation (2 sorties/day to 4ID), AD status correctly stated

### Flagging Gaps

- [ ] **Missing information acknowledged** — items the extract doesn't provide are explicitly flagged (e.g., specific frequencies, unit locations within AO, current strength figures)
- [ ] **Assumptions labeled** — any assumption required to complete a product is clearly identified as an assumption
- [ ] **Placeholders used** — `[CONFIRM with S6]`, `[S1 to provide]`, `[ACTUAL required]` or equivalent notations used where data is unavailable

### Epistemic Markers

- [ ] **Fact/Assessment/Assumption labeling** used where applicable (especially in any enemy analysis)
- [ ] **PIR vs. confirmed information** distinction maintained — 62 DTG assessed at reduced strength is a fact from the extract; 624 BTG employment is a PIR (unknown)

## Scoring Guide

| Section | Weight | Pass Threshold |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| Time Analysis | 20% | Math is correct; 1/3-2/3 applied; DP1 constraint identified |
| WARNO #1 | 30% | All six mission tasks present; correct addressees; no invented facts |
| Planning Timeline | 20% | Fixed events match OPORD; logical sequence; owners assigned |
| Staff Initial Tasks | 20% | All five sections tasked; voice bridge task to S6; PIRs to S2 |
| Accuracy / Intellectual Honesty | 10% | No hallucinated facts; gaps flagged; assumptions labeled |

**Overall Pass:** 80% or higher, with no disqualifying accuracy failures in any section.

**Disqualifying failures:**
- Invented units in task organization
- Invented DTGs presented as OPORD facts
- Missing the tactical voice bridge task (it is an explicit OPORD task with a hard deadline)
- WARNO sent to incomplete addressee list (omitting an attached unit)
- MOPP levels stated incorrectly
